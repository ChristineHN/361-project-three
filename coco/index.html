Coco

Coco is a pompits which is mix of pomeranian and spitz. He loves to play with his toys especially a ball. He barks whenever we have a visitor because he gets so scared. Sometimes he loves to go out but he gets scared easily so he only likes to go out for a walk when there are not a lot of people.
